IT Policies

1. IT Security Policy

  • All systems must be protected with strong passwords (min 12 characters).
  • Mandatory use of company-approved antivirus and firewall.
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) is required for all critical systems.
  • Data encryption is mandatory for laptops and mobile devices.

2. Remote Access Policy

  • Remote access is allowed only via secure VPN.
  • Public Wi-Fi must be avoided unless VPN is active.
  • Employees must log off after work hours when using remote connections.
  • Access logs are regularly monitored by IT security team.

3. Email & Communication Policy

  • Official communication must use company-provided email IDs.
  • No sharing of confidential documents over personal email.
  • Suspicious attachments or phishing emails must be reported immediately.
  • Mass email campaigns require prior approval from IT and Marketing.

4. Software & Hardware Usage Policy

  • Only licensed and IT-approved software may be installed.
  • Unauthorized downloads or torrent usage are strictly prohibited.
  • Hardware issued remains company property and must be returned upon exit.
  • All software updates must be installed within 7 days of release.
